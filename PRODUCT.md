# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Internal factory staff including production workers (reporters), technicians, engineers (QC), and administrators. Often accessing the system via mobile through the LINE app.

## Product Purpose
SFC SMART REPAIR & PM ONLINE. A web application to manage factory repair requests and preventive maintenance (PM) workflows. Success is defined by fast reporting, strict QA (Engineer must close), and easy mobile access for mechanics on the floor.

## Brand Personality
Modern, Premium, and Highly Animated.

## Anti-references
Avoid form-heavy inputs (use dropdowns/clicks instead). Avoid cluttered screens. Avoid confusing, loud, or flashy color schemes. 

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity at a Glance**: Large text and high contrast for mechanics on the factory floor.
2. **Frictionless Input**: Clicks and dropdowns over typing.
3. **Role-Tailored Views**: Mobile-first for reporters/technicians, Desktop-focused for Admins/Engineers.
4. **Premium Feel, Functional Core**: Use smooth animations and modern UI, but never sacrifice ease of use for non-tech-savvy users.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High contrast is required. Large, legible typography is mandatory for on-site use. Must be dead-simple for users who are not tech-savvy.
